Not sure what to order? This is the same spectrum our team uses to match every customer.

Type 1

Straight

Sleek, no curl pattern. Best for silky weaves and bone-straight lace fronts.

Type 2

Wavy

Loose S-shaped waves. Suits body wave and beach wave bundles.

Type 3

Curly

Defined spirals. Deep wave and curly closures hold shape wash after wash.

Type 4

Coily

Tight, springy coils. Kinky curly bundles blend closest to natural 4C hair.
